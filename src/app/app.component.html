Ether Voxel Space

 - Let's create a piece of 3d art togETHer. One voxel at a time.

Micro White Paper

  • World size is limited to 256x256x256 forever
  • Every voxel has it's own x,y,z coordinates (0 to 255), color and owner properties
  • There are 16 available colors (but new can be introduced, to maximum 256 colors/materials)
  • Users can perform only 4 types of actions:
    1. Place (create) a new voxel in chosen position and color
    2. Repaint existing owned voxel
    3. Destroyed owned voxel
    4. Transfer voxel's ownership to someone else
  • The fee for every action is 0.0001 ETH (apart from transaction fees)
  • Contract address: 0x00
